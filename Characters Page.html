ASH KETCHUM

A young trainer who has a goal set and lives in Pallet Town in the Kanto region. His dream is to become a Pokémon Master and be the greatest trainer in the Pokémon world. At a young age, he starts to capture Pokémon and his first Pokémonis Pikachu, who is his loyal companion since day one.

PIKACHU

Pikachu is his first loyal companion and the most iconic Pokémon. It is an electric type, and it is known for its lightning bolt-shaped tail.

GRENINJA

Greninja is a water-type Pokémon that is also called Ash-Greninja. He is called that because of their strong bond together. It is known for its ninja looks with its neck scarf warp around.

TEAM ROCKET

Team Rocket is an iconic enemy in the Pokémon series, and they are known for capturing Pokémons to make a profit out of it. They are also known for trying to capture Ash's Pikachu multiple times and failing.
